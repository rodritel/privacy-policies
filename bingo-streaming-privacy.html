Privacy Policy

Bingo Streaming — Twitch Extension • Last updated: February 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Bingo Streaming ("the Extension") is a Twitch Extension that allows viewers to play bingo during live streams. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal identifying information (PII). We do not collect names, email addresses, passwords, financial data, or any sensitive information.

The Extension processes the following data, provided automatically by Twitch when you interact with it:

  • Twitch User ID — A numeric identifier assigned by Twitch to your account. This is used to assign bingo cards and track your game progress during a session.
  • Channel ID — The numeric identifier of the channel where the Extension is active. This is used to associate game sessions with the correct broadcaster.

We also process the following game-related data:

  • Bingo card assignments (which cards are assigned to your User ID)
  • Game state (called numbers, session status)
  • Bingo claims (when you claim a win)
  • Auto-mark preference (whether numbers are marked automatically)

3. How We Use Your Data

All data is used exclusively to provide the bingo game functionality:

  • Assign and display your bingo cards
  • Track called numbers and mark your cards
  • Validate bingo claims
  • Display game results

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the game.

4. Data Storage & Retention

  • Backend: Game data is stored in Google Sheets via Google Apps Script. This includes session data, card assignments, and game results.
  • Client-side: The Extension stores no data locally on your device. No cookies, localStorage, or similar technologies are used by the viewer component.
  • Retention: Game session data is retained only for the duration of the broadcaster's use. No historical player data is maintained across separate gaming sessions.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties.

Data flows only between:

  • The Twitch Extension (your browser) and our Google Apps Script backend
  • Twitch's PubSub service (for real-time game updates)
  • Twitch's Configuration Service (for persisting broadcaster settings)

6. Third-Party Services

The Extension relies on the following third-party services:

  • Twitch Interactive — Authentication, PubSub messaging, and Configuration Service (Twitch Privacy Policy)
  • Google Apps Script / Google Sheets — Backend processing and data storage (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Google Fonts — Font delivery (no personal data transmitted)

7. Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age. The Extension operates within Twitch's platform, which requires users to be at least 13 years old as per Twitch's Terms of Service.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Stop using the Extension at any time by not interacting with it
  • Request information about what data we hold related to your Twitch User ID
  • Request deletion of your game data

To exercise these rights, contact us via the methods listed in Section 10.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
